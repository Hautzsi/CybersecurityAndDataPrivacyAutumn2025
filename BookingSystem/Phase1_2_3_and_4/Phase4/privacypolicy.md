This Privacy Policy explains how Booking System collects, uses, and protects personal data when users interact with our booking system. We are committed to complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

When you register or make a reservation, we may collect:

- Password (securely hashed using bcrypt)
- Birthdate
- User role (e.g., Reserver)
- Reservation details: resource name, start and end time
- Consent to Terms of Service

### 3. How We Use Your Data
We use your data to:
- Create and manage user accounts
- Process and display reservations

### 4. Legal Basis for Processing
We process personal data based on:

- Contractual necessity (e.g., managing reservations)

### 5. Data Retention
We retain personal data only as long as necessary:
