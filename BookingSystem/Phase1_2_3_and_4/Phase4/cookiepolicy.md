# Cookie Policy – Booking System

### 1. What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our booking system. They help us provide core functionality, improve user experience, and analyze usage.

### 2. Types of Cookies We Use
- Essential cookies
Required for the system to function (e.g., session management, login state).
- Preference cookies
Store user settings such as language or role selection.
- Analytics cookies
Collect anonymized data about usage patterns to improve performance.

### 3. How We Use Cookies
We use cookies to:
- Keep you logged in securely
- Remember your booking preferences
- Display available resources correctly

### 4. Third‑Party Cookies
Not included in this version

### 5. Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- Block or delete cookies
- Receive alerts before cookies are stored
Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect system functionality.

### 6. Consent
By registering or using the booking system, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You may withdraw consent by adjusting your browser settings.
